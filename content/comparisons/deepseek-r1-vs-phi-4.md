# How to Optimize Your Website for DeepSeek-R1 vs Phi-4

As AI platforms evolve, optimizing your content for different models requires understanding their unique characteristics and preferences. This comprehensive guide compares optimization strategies for **DeepSeek-R1** and **Phi-4**, helping you maximize visibility across both platforms.

### DeepSeek-R1 (DeepSeek)
- **Type**: Reasoning Model
- **Key Strengths**: mathematics, logic, step-by-step reasoning
- **Release Year**: 2025

### Phi-4 (Microsoft)
- **Type**: Small Language Model
- **Key Strengths**: efficiency, edge computing, specialized tasks
- **Release Year**: 2025
