### Grok 4 (xAI)

- **Key Strengths**: real-time data, humor, current events
- **Release Year**: 2025

### ChatGPT 5 Mini (OpenAI)
- **Type**: Lightweight Model
- **Key Strengths**: speed, cost-efficiency, basic tasks
- **Release Year**: 2025
